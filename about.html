About Us

In February 2005, Aurora Danga Tamondong established ADT Market Research Services (ADT MRS), as an independent proprietorship. It meets all the needs for doing market research, including providing tabulation services and field staff. With over thirty years of expertise, founder Aurora Danga Tamondong had a broad foundation in the field of market research. Her daughter, Ofelia Tamondong-Maglalang, is currently in charge of ADT MRS.

10 permanent workers now work for the firm, located at Unit 102-103 One Lourdes Place #126 Road 1, Bagong Pag-asa, Quezon City, 1105. 80 contract/project-based field interviewers and four field managers/supervisors are available for deployment anywhere in Luzon. We also have a team of field interviewers operating in the Philippines nationwide, including Cebu and Davao.

ADT MRS guarantees that all services are provided in accordance with high-quality standards and upholds inviolable confidentiality about all information obtained from its reputable and skilled staff. Our field services may be tailored to meet the customer's specific needs and are assured to be highly compliant with MORES standards.
